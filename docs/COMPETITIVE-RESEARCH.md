# AI Receptionist Competitive Research

## 1. Fin AI (Intercom)
- **Setup:** 14-day trial, self-serve, integrated with Intercom
- **Dashboard:** Unified inbox, tickets, analytics (Intercom suite)
- **Voice:** Text/chat-focused, not voice AI
- **Pricing:** $29/mo + $0.99/resolution
- **Edge:** Resolution-based pricing, existing Intercom ecosystem

## 2. Smith.ai
- **Setup:** Onboarding call required, 2-3 days to live
- **Dashboard:** Call monitoring, CRM sync (HubSpot/Salesforce), custom intake
- **Voice:** Live humans 24/7 (US-based, not AI)
- **Pricing:** $300-2,100/mo (30-300 calls), $8.50-11.50/call overage
- **Edge:** Human-first, 30-day guarantee, 24/7 live agents

## 3. Ruby Receptionists
- **Setup:** Sales consultation required, less self-serve
- **Dashboard:** Portal for routing, hours, call logs
- **Voice:** Human receptionists (US-based)
- **Pricing:** $300-800/mo, per-call model
- **Edge:** Premium human service, trusted brand

## 4. Synthflow
- **Setup:** No-code builder, same-day live
- **Dashboard:** Visual flow editor, telephony, CRM integrations
- **Voice:** ElevenLabs, Azure TTS
- **Pricing:** $0.07-0.08/min (~$15K/year enterprise)
- **Edge:** No-code visual builder, white-label, built-in telephony

## 5. Retell AI
- **Setup:** API-first, instant signup, $10 free credits
- **Dashboard:** Modular calculator, analytics, simulations, 10 free knowledge bases
- **Voice:** Cartesia/Minimax/ElevenLabs ($0.015/min), low latency
- **Pricing:** $0.07+/min + LLM ($0.003-0.05) + voice, pay-as-you-go
- **Edge:** Full transparency, developer-focused, modular pricing, no platform fees

## 6. Bland AI
- **Setup:** API/developer-focused, technical setup
- **Dashboard:** Developer tools, webhooks, voice tuning
- **Voice:** Proprietary models, custom tone/rhythm control
- **Pricing:** $0.09/min (800ms latency reported)
- **Edge:** Own AI stack, no OpenAI/Anthropic dependency

---

**Key Takeaways:**
- Human (Smith/Ruby) vs AI divide | Developer (Retell/Bland) vs no-code (Synthflow)
- Time to live: Retell (instant) > Synthflow (same day) > Smith/Ruby (2-3 days)
- Pricing: Per-resolution (Fin) | Per-call (Smith/Ruby) | Per-minute (rest)
- Voice leaders: Bland (proprietary), Retell (low latency)
