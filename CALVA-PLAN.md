## Mission
Build the best AI receptionist product for ANY business. Restaurants, law firms, medical offices, plumbers, salons, auto shops, real estate — every vertical where missed calls = lost money.

## Why We Win
1. **Price** — $297/mo vs $500-2000/mo competitors (Smith.ai, Ruby, Dialzara)
2. **Always on** — 24/7/365, no sick days, no hold times
3. **Actually books appointments** — Not just answers, but converts callers to customers
4. **Industry-specific** — Pre-built templates that know the business, not generic scripts
5. **Setup in minutes** — Not weeks of onboarding

## Lean Tech Stack (Phase 1)
| Component | Choice | Cost |
|-----------|--------|------|
| Telephony | Twilio (existing) | ~$0.02/min |
| LLM | Claude Haiku/Sonnet (structured prompts) | ~$0.01/call |
| TTS | Edge TTS (free) → ElevenLabs when revenue | $0/mo |
| STT | Deepgram or Whisper | Free tier / minimal |
| Hosting | Mac mini or $5 VPS | $0-5/mo |
| Scheduling | Cal.com (free tier) | $0 |
| Payments | Stripe | 2.9% + $0.30 |
| Landing page | Static site (GitHub Pages or Vercel) | $0 |
| CRM | HubSpot free | $0 |

**Per-call cost: ~$0.05-0.10** → 97%+ margin at $297/mo
