## Executive Summary

The AI receptionist market is fiercely competitive with players spanning three distinct categories: **enterprise customer service platforms** (Fin AI), **human-AI hybrid services** (Smith.ai, Ruby), and **developer-focused voice AI platforms** (Synthflow, Retell AI). Key findings:

### Critical Takeaways

1. **Pricing is all over the map**: From $0.07/minute (Retell AI) to $720/month for 200 minutes (Ruby), with Fin AI at $0.99/resolution requiring expensive Intercom subscriptions ($29-$495+/month per seat).

2. **Self-service is king for SMBs**: Synthflow and Retell AI enable instant signup and deployment. Fin AI requires Intercom platform adoption. Ruby/Smith.ai require sales calls and onboarding.

3. **Voice quality varies dramatically**: Human services (Ruby) deliver unmatched warmth but at 10x the cost. AI platforms (Synthflow, Retell) claim natural conversations with sub-second latency. Fin AI has mixed reviews on voice quality.

4. **Enterprise = Integrations + Compliance + Scale**: HIPAA compliance, Salesforce/Zendesk integrations, and multi-channel support (voice, email, SMS, WhatsApp, Slack) are table stakes for "enterprise-level" positioning.

5. **Calva's opportunity**: Fast self-service onboarding + enterprise-grade features at mid-market pricing could capture massive market share between expensive human services and bare-bones developer platforms.
