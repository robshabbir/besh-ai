📞 Call Forwarding Setup Guide

Connect your existing business number to Calva AI

Keep Your Existing Number!

Your customers can keep calling your current business number. When you're busy or can't answer, calls automatically forward to your Calva AI receptionist.

How it works: Set up conditional call forwarding so calls only go to Calva when you don't answer after a few rings.

A AT&T

▼

Method 1: Forward When Busy or Unanswered

  1. Dial *61*[CALVA-NUMBER]*11#
  2. Press the Call button
  3. Wait for confirmation message
  4. Test by having someone call you while your phone is off
Note: The *11# sets a 10-second delay before forwarding. Adjust to *21# for 20 seconds or *31# for 30 seconds.

Method 2: Online Account Settings

  1. Log in to att.com/my
  2. Go to "My wireless" > "Manage my device"
  3. Select "Call forwarding"
  4. Enable "Forward when busy" or "Forward when unanswered"
  5. Enter your Calva number
  6. Save changes
💡 Tip: To disable forwarding, dial ##61# and press Call.

V Verizon

▼

Method 1: Conditional Call Forwarding

  1. Dial *71[CALVA-NUMBER]
  2. Press the Call button
  3. Wait for confirmation tone or message
  4. Hang up
Note: *71 forwards calls when you don't answer. By default, it waits about 15 seconds (4 rings) before forwarding.

Method 2: My Verizon App

  1. Open the My Verizon app
  2. Tap "Devices" > Select your phone
  3. Tap "Manage device" > "Controls"
  4. Select "Call Forwarding"
  5. Enable "Forward when unreachable"
  6. Enter your Calva number and save
💡 Tip: To turn off forwarding, dial *73 and press Call.

T T-Mobile

▼

Method 1: Forward When Unanswered

  1. Dial **61*[CALVA-NUMBER]**10#
  2. Press the Call button
  3. You'll hear a confirmation message
  4. Hang up when confirmed
Note: The **10# sets a 10-second timeout. Use **15# for 15 seconds, **20# for 20 seconds, or **30# for 30 seconds.

Method 2: T-Mobile App

  1. Open the T-Mobile app
  2. Tap "Account" > "Line settings"
  3. Select "Call forwarding"
  4. Enable "When unanswered"
  5. Enter your Calva number
  6. Set delay (10-30 seconds) and save
💡 Tip: To disable, dial ##61# and press Call.

S Spectrum Mobile

▼

Call Forwarding Setup

  1. Dial *61*[CALVA-NUMBER]*11#
  2. Press the Call button
  3. Wait for the confirmation message
  4. Hang up
Note: Spectrum Mobile uses Verizon's network, so Verizon codes work. *61 forwards when you don't answer.

Alternative: Spectrum Account

  1. Log in to mobile.spectrum.com
  2. Select your phone line
  3. Go to "Settings" > "Call Settings"
  4. Select "Call Forwarding"
  5. Enable and enter your Calva number
  6. Save changes
💡 Tip: To turn off, dial ##61# and press Call.

Testing Your Setup

  1. Call your business number from another phone
  2. Let it ring without answering
  3. Calva AI should pick up after the delay you set
  4. Try a conversation to test the AI
💡 Pro Tip: Set the forwarding delay to 15-20 seconds so you have time to answer if you're available, but customers don't wait too long.
