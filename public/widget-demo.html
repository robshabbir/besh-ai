How to Install

Add this single line of code to your website's HTML, right before the closing </body> tag:

<script src="https://your-domain.com/widget.js?key=YOUR_API_KEY"></script>

Replace YOUR_API_KEY with your actual API key from your Calva dashboard.

Features

  • ✓ Instant responses - AI answers in seconds
  • ✓ Same knowledge base - Uses your business info from voice calls
  • ✓ Session persistence - Conversation saved across page views
  • ✓ Mobile responsive - Works perfectly on all devices
  • ✓ Customizable - Matches your brand colors (coming soon)
  • ✓ Rate limited - 60 messages per minute to prevent abuse

💡 Pro Tip

The chat widget uses the same AI and knowledge base as your voice receptionist. Update your business info in the dashboard, and both channels stay in sync automatically.
