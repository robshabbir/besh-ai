Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address, business name, password (hashed), and phone number when you create an account.

Call Data: Phone numbers of callers, call duration, transcripts of AI conversations, caller-provided information (name, phone, service requests), and call recordings when enabled.

Usage Data: Dashboard activity, feature usage, and browser information for improving the Service.

Payment Information: Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the AI receptionist service
  • To process calls and deliver transcripts/summaries
  • To send transactional emails (verification, password reset, call summaries)
  • To improve our AI conversation quality
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Twilio: For phone call handling and SMS
  • Google (Gemini): For AI conversation processing (call content is processed but not stored by Google for training)
  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Your configured integrations: CRM webhooks, HubSpot, etc. (only when you enable them)

4. Data Retention

Call transcripts and recordings are retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days. Account data is deleted 30 days after account closure upon request. You can export your data at any time via the dashboard CSV export.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Hashed passwords (bcrypt)
  • Rate limiting on all endpoints
  • Input validation and XSS protection
  • Session security with HTTP-only cookies

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your call data (CSV)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Update your account information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Cookies

We use essential session cookies for authentication. We do not use third-party tracking cookies. Analytics, when enabled, use privacy-respecting tools.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users.

10. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact privacy@calva.ai.
