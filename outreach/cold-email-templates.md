## Outreach Best Practices
1. **Call first, email second** — proves the point (they didn't answer)
2. **Lead with the demo number** — hearing is believing
3. **$99/month is the hook** — cheaper than one missed job
4. **Free 7-day trial** — removes all risk
5. **Set up in 10 minutes** — no friction
6. **Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9am** — best send times for small biz owners
7. **Follow up 3x** — Day 1, Day 3, Day 7
