# Calva Outreach Playbook — Get First 10 Customers
*Autonomous execution by Santa | Budget: $0*

## Strategy: "I Called You" Method

### Step 1: Find businesses that miss calls
- Scrape Google Maps for small businesses in NYC
- Call them at random times (or note that they went to voicemail)
- Document: "Called at 2:15pm Tuesday, got voicemail"

### Step 2: Cold email with proof
- "I called you at 2:15pm and no one picked up"
- Include demo number: (929) 755-7288
- Offer: $99/mo, 7-day free trial, 10-minute setup

### Step 3: Follow-up sequence
- Day 1: Email
- Day 3: Follow-up email
- Day 5: LinkedIn DM or SMS
- Day 7: Final email with urgency

### Step 4: Close on the call
- "Call (929) 755-7288 — hear it yourself"
- "I'll set it up in 10 minutes"
- "$99/month, cancel anytime"
- "Free for 7 days"

## Daily Targets
- 20 new leads identified
- 20 cold emails sent
- 5 follow-ups sent
- 1-2 demos/trials started

## Weekly Pipeline
- Week 1: 100 leads → 20 contacts → 5 demos → 2 trials
- Week 2: 100 more leads → follow-ups → 3-5 trials → 1-2 paid
- Week 3: Iterate, referrals start → 5+ paying customers
- Week 4: $500-1000 MRR

## HubSpot CRM Workflow

1. **Lead Identified** — scraped, added to list
2. **Contacted** — first email/call made

4. **Trial Started** — 7-day free trial active

## Verticals Priority (NYC)
1. Plumbers/HVAC — highest urgency, highest ticket
2. Law firms (solo/small) — can't miss client calls
3. Dental offices — appointment-heavy
4. Auto repair — always on the phone
5. Restaurants — reservations, hours questions
6. Salons — booking is their business
